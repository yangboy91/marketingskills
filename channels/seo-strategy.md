## On-Page SEO Checklist

For every blog post:
- Target keyword in H1 (exact or close match)
- Keyword in first 100 words
- Meta title 60 chars max, includes keyword
- Meta description 155 chars max, keyword plus CTA
- 3-5 internal links to other posts and homepage
- Image alt text includes keyword
- FAQ section at bottom (captures People Also Ask)
- Word count: 1,200-1,800 words
- Schema markup: Article type
